Privacy Policy

Cards Control - NFC & Business Cards App

Last updated: December 7, 2024

Summary: Cards Control respects your privacy. We only collect data necessary for the app to function and never sell it to third parties.

1. Data Collected

Cards Control collects the following types of data:

  • Account data: Email address and name (during registration)
  • Business cards: Information you enter (name, phone, email, etc.)
  • NFC tags: Content of tags you read or write
  • Photos: Images you add to your cards (stored securely)
  • Usage data: Anonymized statistics to improve the app

2. Use of Data

Your data is used to:

  • Create and manage your digital business cards
  • Sync your data across your devices
  • Provide NFC features (reading, writing, emulation)
  • Improve user experience
  • Send you important notifications (if permitted)

3. Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely:

  • Hosted on Firebase (Google Cloud Platform)
  • Data encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication via Firebase Auth
  • Sensitive data stored locally with encryption

4. Data Sharing

We never sell your personal data. Your data may only be shared:

  • With your explicit consent (sharing a business card)
  • With our service providers (Firebase, Google Analytics)
  • If required by law

5. App Permissions

Cards Control requests the following permissions:

  • NFC: To read and write NFC tags
  • Camera: To scan business cards and QR codes
  • Storage: To save your profile photos
  • Internet: For data synchronization
  • Contacts: To import/export contacts (optional)

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all your data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Object to data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.

7. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. After account deletion, all your data is erased within 30 days.

8. Changes

This policy may be updated. We will notify you of any significant changes via the app or email.

9. Contact

For any questions about this privacy policy:

Email: contact@cards-control.app
